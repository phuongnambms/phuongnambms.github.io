MECHANICAL FIXED TEMPERATURE HEAT DETECTOR (HORING LIH AH-9920)

  • October 27, 2022
  • Phuong Nam

Feature

  • This heat detector works on the principle of heat induction, with the thermocouple connected to a metal disc, being approved by UL.
  • There are different temperature thresholds to choose from. Made of high-quality materials, perfect contact, enhanced operability even in bad environments.
  • This device can be installed in environments with high temperatures or large temperature changes such as kitchens, hotels or boiler houses.
  • The device structure is tight, not affected by moisture, dust or insect intrusion.
  • The equipment has passed strict quality checks by automatic testing equipment.
  • Guaranteed quality and high reliability.
  • Professionally designed, when heated, the thermostat remains undistorted, ensuring its function.

Specification

Model 2, 3 or 4 wires (4-wire type has alarm function 0.8A (30VDC), 0.4A (125VAC)
Thermal setting 70°C or other selections
Voltage Range 12 ~ 30V DC
Alarm Current @24V DC 470Ω 40mA (2 or 3-wire), 35mA (4-wire)
Alarm Temperature Comply to EN54
Ambient Temperature -10°C ~ +55°C
